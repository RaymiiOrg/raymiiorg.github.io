This little snippet turns off Sleep on an OS X machine, either iMac or Macbook.
Normally when a Mac is not used it goes in sleep mode, this turns that off.

To turn off sleep mode:

    
    
    systemsetup -setsleep Off
    

Or to set an other time (5 minutes for example):

    
    
    systemsetup -setsleep 5
